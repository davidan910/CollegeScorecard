College Decision Scorecard

A Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) tool to help you evaluate your college options objectively — by weighing what actually matters to you.

What is MCDA?

The MCDA scorecard helps you cut through gut feelings and cognitive bias by putting real numbers to your values. Instead of going back and forth in your head, you score each school systematically and let the composite score surface what your gut already knows — or challenge what you assumed.

Step 1 — List
List every criterion that matters to you: cost, strength of program, opportunity, living situation, or whatever your top values are.
Step 2 — Weight
Assign each criterion a weight — Low, Medium, or High — based on how much it personally matters to you right now.
Step 3 — Grade & Score
Score each school 1–5 per criterion. The tool multiplies score × weight and adds it all up into a composite score for each school.
